Restaurant Description
Sit at the bar at Sushi Fix in Wayzata, and, in addition to enjoying unbelievably fresh fish and standard-setting wagyu beef nibbles, you’ll inevitably make friends with your neighbors. The conviviality starts with owner Billy Tserenbat, a master of raucous ceremony. Whether you’re sitting at the sushi bar waiting for your George Clooney roll bursting with big eye tuna and unagi sauce or at the whisky bar sipping down the best stash of Japanese labels in town, it’s almost impossible to leave without having had the time of your life. If the restaurant’s signature Shot-Ski makes an appearance, with multiple shot glasses fixed to a waterski, those new friends could be yours for life.
